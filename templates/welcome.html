Health Stats Tracker

The all in one app for tracking your health

Thank you for visiting this website. If you would like to use the features, please either sign up, if you do not have an account, or log in.

This web app can allow users to enter health statistics that they would like to keep track of. Users can input different statistics and check how what their statistics look like over a long period of time. This is meant to promote healthy living and habits, since users can check on their own personal health via this web app. It's as easy as signing up or logging in!

Why does this app matter?

This app is useful because it helps users anywhere keep track of their health. Physical health is a very important part of life; without keeping your body functioning well, most aspects of life will suffer. Therefore, this app is useful because it's one place for a user to make sure they are staying healthy by keeping track of their statistics, like weight, BMI, food intake etc. This app is meant to promote healthy living.
